# Examples

The best way to understand the Skillbook Format is to see it in action. These repositories
implement the specification and serve as reference implementations.

## Example Skillbooks

### EU AI Act
The EU Artificial Intelligence Act — full regulatory text with risk classification guidance.
- **Repo:** [skillbooks-ai/skillbook-eu-ai-act](https://github.com/skillbooks-ai/skillbook-eu-ai-act)
- **Demonstrates:** Verified sources, tag-based lookup, regulatory content

### EPA 608
EPA Section 608 certification exam prep — refrigerant handling, recovery, and regulations.
- **Repo:** [skillbooks-ai/skillbook-epa-608](https://github.com/skillbooks-ai/skillbook-epa-608)
- **Demonstrates:** Exam prep format, public domain source material, practical Q&A structure

### Shakespeare Complete Works
All 37 plays broken into act/scene structure with scholarly annotations.
- **Repo:** [skillbooks-ai/skillbook-shakespeare-complete](https://github.com/skillbooks-ai/skillbook-shakespeare-complete)
- **Demonstrates:** Large-scale literary content (725 scenes), deep section nesting

## This Repository

This specification repository is itself a skillbook. Browse its structure to see the format
in action — the `SKILL.md`, section `00-overview.md` files, content pages, `package.json`
config, and navigation links all follow the conventions described in the spec.
